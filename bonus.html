Miami Macaw

Sport: Football

Macaws are a group of New World parrots that are long-tailed and often colorful. They are popular in aviculture or as companion parrots, although there are conservation concerns about several species in the wild.

  1. The largest macaws are the hyacinth, Buffon's (great green) and green-winged macaws.
  2. There are 19 species of macaws, including extinct and critically endangered species.
  3. Some foods eaten by macaws in certain regions in the wild are said to contain toxic or caustic substances which they are able to digest.
